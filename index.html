Steps to prepare special maggi

  1. Fry onion and tomato in butter
  2. Add spices like-salt,coriander and add green chilli 😀
  3. Add water to fried tomato onion paste and let it boil 😆
  4. Put maggi into the boiled water 😋
  5. Add maggi masala and let the maggi ready 😊
  6. Once, maggi is ready add a slice of butter to it 😉
